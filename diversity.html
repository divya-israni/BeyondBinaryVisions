What is Diversity and Inclusion?

Diversity and Inclusion (D&I) celebrates diverse experiences and perspectives, encompassing visible and unseen differences. Inclusion creates an environment where everyone feels valued. Championing D&I leads to creativity, better decision-making, and effective problem-solving. Sustained commitment and awareness of biases are crucial. Ultimately, D&I fosters fair, respectful, and productive spaces.

Promoting Diversity and Inclusion

Embrace diversity and inclusion for a stronger, more innovative community. Valuing every perspective leads to better outcomes and a culture of respect. Let's create spaces where everyone can thrive. Many big companies, such as Johnson & Johnson, Mastercard, and Marriott International, have their own diversity and inclusion statmenets. Companies around the world are beginning to follow this approach and build a more inclusive environment for everyone.

Resources

These resources are free and centered towards underrepresented groups. Their goal is further the skills and knowledge of these individuals, while giving them a safe and supporitive community to thrive in.

Girls Who Code

An international nonprofit that seeks to further the skills and increase the amount of women in computer science. GWC offers many different paths to enhance your coding skills and learn about different branches of CS.

Kode with Klossy

A nonprofit organization that empoowers women, gender nonconforming, and trans students in technology thorugh coding camps and scholarships.

Code Liberation

A nonprofit organization that provides free beginner-friendly game development workshops and resources for women and non-binary individuals. The organization seeks to promote gender diversity in the gaming industry.

ChickTech

A nonprofit organization that supports women in STEM through hands-on workshops, mentoring, and community-building initiatives.

Black Girls Who Code

A nonprofit organization that empowers black girls and women in technology and helps them pursue STEM related careers through various resources.
