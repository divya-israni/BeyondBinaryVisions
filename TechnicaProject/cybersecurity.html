What is Cybersecurity?

Protecting networks, devices, and data from cyberthreats and ensuring their security. It refers to the practices people use to protect computer systems and networks from cyber attacks. CIA triad is used to discuss cybersecurity: CIA stands for confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

Education/Experience Needed

  • Bachelor’s degree: Computer science, Cybersecurity, or Information Technology
  • Hard skills: Operating system knowledge, Network security, Programming and scripting, Cryptography
  • Soft skills: Problem solving, Critical thinking, Attention to detail, Risk assessment, Teamwork
  • Recommended programming languages: Java, JavaScript, Python, SQL, PHP, PowerShell, and C

Careers in Cybersecurity

Security Consultant
